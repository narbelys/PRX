About Us

PRX Control Solutions is a healthtech company that got started from more than 10 years of experience of its founders working with pharmacies and health plans.


The company was founded in 2012 when the founders noticed that health plans were lacking from tools and resources for controlling the prescription and dispensing process of pharmaceutical treatments.


PRX’s first solution is PCA Audit, an app that detects 35 types of anomalies in prescriptions and pharmacy invoices. The app has an algorithm that compares more than 20 variables in each claim it analyzes, recommending the company’s clients if they should approve or reimburse a treatment to the patient or service provider. PCA Audit has multiple uses, like for example, for auditing pharmacy claims or for the EPA (electronic previous authorization) process for approving treatments in pharmacies.


In the first two years of operations PRX Control Solutions saved it’s clients more than US$15MM
