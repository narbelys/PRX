Prescription Intelligence

Business intelligence applied to the pharmaceutical industry

THE COMPANY

PRX Control Solutions is a company that designs and develops apps for the health and pharmaceutical industry, focused on prescription control and health management.

Our products and services are designed and developed for prescription control and health management.





THE PROBLEM

In PRX we solve 3 problems

INSUFFICIENT CONTROL

There's insufficient control over medication consumption from health insurance providers.

INSUFFICIENT SUPERVISION

There's insufficient supervision of patient's healths from healthcare providers or pharmacies.





More than 50% of prescriptions in the United States aren’t taken as prescribed, costing the country up to $289b and 125.000 deaths a year.



(Source: http://www.theatlantic.com/health/archive/2012/09/the-289-billion-cost-of-medication- noncompliance-and-what-to-do-about-it/262222/)
